About

From solo research engineer to AI agency

Nice to meet you here! I'm Stefan, a Machine Learning Engineer and founder of a AI agency. Since 2018 I've been building and shipping machine learning systems and hold a Master's degree in Big Data and Machine Learning.

Today I stay hands-on with the core modeling work while coordinating a small network of trusted collaborators and specialists for design, data engineering, MLOps, and supporting development. This gives you the focus and accountability of working with one person, with the capacity and skill coverage of a small, flexible team.

We specialize in training language models from scratch for tailored applications, integrating multiple modalities, and conducting pretraining and fine-tuning of various large language models. We also work with knowledge graphs, where we build them from raw text, extract meaningful representations, and tackle link prediction and classification tasks, as well as computer vision for various applications. All solutions are production-ready and easy to deploy.

How We Work

A focused core with a flexible network of specialists

I remain your primary point of contact and technical lead on every project. When needed, I bring in vetted collaborators from my network for complementary roles such as data engineering, frontend/backend development, DevOps/MLOps, and UX.

  • You work directly with me on architecture, modeling, and key decisions.
  • A small project team is assembled around your specific needs.
  • You get access to niche skills such as RAG pipelines, multi-agent systems, GPU optimization, and domain-specific modeling.
  • You always know who is working on what, and can scale up or down quickly as your priorities change.
